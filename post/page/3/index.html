Sondesh

Introduction Sondesh is a popular Bengali sweet that holds a special place in Indian cuisine, particularly in the state of West Bengal. It is a delicacy made from fresh paneer (Indian cottage cheese) or chhena, which is obtained by curdling milk and then separating the whey. The paneer is then kneaded and mixed with sugar, ghee (clarified butter), and various flavorings to create a smooth, creamy, and sweet dessert. Sondesh is enjoyed during festivals, celebrations, and special occasions in Bengali households.
